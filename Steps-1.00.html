Project overview

 

Airbnb has transformed the travel landscape by offering a platform that connects hosts with guests from across the globe. This comprehensive Airbnb analysis for ten major cities (2008-2021) is embodied in a Power BI dashboard, serving as a dynamic tool for scrutinizing rental listings. It delivers valuable insights into market dynamics, host efficiency, and property performance, all aimed at enhancing revenue. Acting as a decision-support system for the company, hosts, and customers, the dashboard empowers hosts to optimize their listings, enabling guests to make well-informed choices that encourage repeat bookings and drive revenue growth. By leveraging Data Science and integrating Predictive Analysis, the dashboard not only mirrors the current landscape but also forecasts future trends in review scores.
