Case 1: Increase Revenue by Improving Review Scores
  

·         Objective: Enhance review scores to increase listing visibility and booking rates, ultimately boosting revenue.
 

·          Positive Insight: Listings with a higher score in cleanliness see an average revenue increase. Cleanliness strongly correlates with higher review scores, impacting guest satisfaction and repeat bookings. From 2012 to 2014, Paris maintained a cleanliness rating of 9.34/10, with a yearly improvement of 0.61%. This increase in cleanliness led to a monthly revenue of $58K, with a yearly growth of 2.36%, positively influencing guest satisfaction and encouraging repeat bookings.



   Paris from 2012 - 2014



·         Negative Insight: Listings showing a year-over-year decline in location review scores experience a decrease in revenue. Negative perceptions of location can severely impact the attractiveness of listings. From 2014 to 2018, Sydney experienced a Location YoY decrease of -0.37%, with a significant drop in Revenue per month to $111K, marking a YoY decrease of -5.37%. This underscores the crucial role of location in influencing guest preferences.

   

    


    Sydney from 2014-2018

  

     ROI: Investing in improving property cleanliness and addressing location-related issues could lead to a potential 20% increase in annual revenue, given that improved review scores enhance listing competitiveness and booking frequency.
