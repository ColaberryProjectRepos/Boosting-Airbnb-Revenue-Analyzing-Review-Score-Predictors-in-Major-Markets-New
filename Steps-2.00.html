 Executive Summary


  

 

Background


Airbnb has revolutionized the travel industry by providing a platform for hosts to rent out their properties to guests from around the world. The quality of guest experiences can significantly vary based on numerous factors, affecting overall satisfaction and repeat bookings.

 




Problem Statement



Airbnb listings with higher review scores attract more guests, generate higher revenue, and enhance host credibility. However, many hosts struggle to understand which factors most significantly impact their scores. This analysis addresses this gap by identifying the key predictors of review scores and offering predictive insights that can help hosts improve their service quality and listing attractiveness.




Objectives

 

Implement a dynamic Power BI dashboard to visualize data trends and predict future review scores based on factors such as cleanliness, check-in process, communication, location, and overall experience. This model aims to assist hosts in identifying areas for improvement to boost their revenue and help guests in making informed decisions.
