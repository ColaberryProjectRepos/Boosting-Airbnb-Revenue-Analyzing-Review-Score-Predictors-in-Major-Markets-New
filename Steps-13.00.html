Creating Additional Tables- DimDatetable & Refresh table 13


a) Create DimDatetable

1.     In Table view, click on New table. 
2.     Name the table as DimDateTable.
3.     Enter the Dax formula to create DimDateTable
DimDateTable = CALENDAR(DATE(2008,01,01), DATE(2021, 12, 31)).
4.   Now click on new column to add a column Year, Month, Month Name Short, Week, Day of Week with the following DAX formula respectively.
·         Year = YEAR('DimDateTable'[Date].[Date])
·         Month = MONTH('DimDateTable'[Date])
·         Month Name Short = FORMAT([Date],"MMM")
·         Week = WEEKDAY('DimDateTable'[Date])
·         Day of Week = FORMAT([Date], "ddd")
5.   Sort Month Name Short based on Month and Sort Day of Week based on Week



Create Refresh Table



1. On Power BI Desktop ribbon, Click on Transform data.
 2. Click on New Source button and select Blank query from the list and click on Connect.
 3. Under properties in the Name box type in 'Refresh Date'.
4. On the DAX formula bar type ' =DateTime.LocalNow()'.
5. Click on Convert to Table button.
6. After the refresh table pops up, rename the column by right clicking and choosing rename from the selection.
7.     Click on Close & apply button and save
