Data Sources

 

The dataset utilized in this project was sourced from Kaggle, a premier online platform renowned for its comprehensive repository of high-quality datasets. Kaggle is esteemed for fostering data science initiatives by providing access to a vast array of data across numerous fields. Its extensive resources enable researchers to perform in-depth analyses and derive significant insights into their specific research questions. The Airbnb dataset was meticulously curated and anonymized to ensure privacy and accuracy. Advanced techniques were then applied to correlate rental listings with various factors such as location, property type, and reviews, providing insights into the dynamics of the vacation rental market.

Data Features

 

The Airbnb dataset comprises numerous features that capture essential aspects of property listings. These features include city, neighborhood, rental price, Reviews on   cleanliness, check-in, communication, and location. By examining the relationships between these features, we can understand how different variables influence listing prices and occupancy rates. This analysis is crucial for identifying trends, improving property management, and making informed decisions in the rental market.
